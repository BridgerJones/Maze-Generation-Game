Move through the maze and find your way to the exit tile. Everyone starts with 100,000 points. You lose points as time acrues. You have a few tools at your disposal. However, you will lose additional points per second every time you use a hint or if you reveal the path. Make sure to turn off your tools to conserve points. When you press "h", the next best move will appear if it is within 1 tile in each of your cardinal directions. If you get too far off the path, you may need to use "p" to reveal the path. Make use of breadcrumbs with "b" to keep track of where you are going. You can toggle them on and off as much as you like without penalty.
